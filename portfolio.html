Steps for Creating a Personal Website

1. Set a Clear Goal For Your Personal Website

Before starting to think about content and what to write, it is worth outlining some goals for your personal website. Why are you creating one, and what do you want to achieve?

Many people often focus on trying to build a social media presence, but it is just as important to decide on the purpose of your personal website first this will then inform the content you share on other channels.
