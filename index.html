Steps for Creating a Personal Website

1. Set a Clear Goal For Your Personal Website

Before starting to think about content and what to write, it is worth outlining some goals for your personal website. Why are you creating one, and what do you want to achieve?

Many people often focus on trying to build a social media presence, but it is just as important to decide on the purpose of your personal website first this will then inform the content you share on other channels.

2. Add Your Website is Content

Add static content first: We would recommend adding home page and about and a contact me page with details about yourself early on get these pages up and running before anything else.

Showcase the best of your work: Websites can serve as an online portfolio or display samples of work and link elsewhere. Add a selection of the projects that you’re most proud of!

3.Get Your Website Seen

Social media marketing: It is important to start building your presence across multiple social channels, so add your website in your social profiles, tweet and post about it, and share it with your job search materials. Keep it updated with new information

Add relevant links: Seeking out links back to your personal website from trustworthy and noteworthy publications isn it just good for publicity it is great for helping your website rank for relevant terms in search engine results, too!
